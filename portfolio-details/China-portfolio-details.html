Bangladesh-China Youth Camp 2018

During my journey to Kunming, China, I had the privilege to participate in an international youth camp hosted by the Chinese Embassy. This program allowed me to train with Chinese masters, practice traditional Taolu forms, and perform Wushu demonstrations on stage with both Bangladeshi and Chinese students.

The tour focused on advancing skills in Changquan, Daoshu, Kungfu Fan, and Taijiquan. Training sessions were followed by a vibrant cultural exchange program, where we performed live stage shows demonstrating the discipline, artistry, and beauty of traditional Chinese martial arts.

Adapting to an entirely new training environment with foreign masters and techniques was challenging. Daily practice sessions demanded physical endurance, quick learning, and synchronization with international teammates.

  • Completed intensive training under Chinese Wushu masters
  • Performed Taijiquan stage show with Anoy (BKSP) in front of a live audience
  • Demonstrated traditional weapon forms (Daoshu, Konfu Fan)
  • Represented Bangladesh in an international cultural exchange program
  • Built teamwork and discipline through cross-cultural martial arts practice
