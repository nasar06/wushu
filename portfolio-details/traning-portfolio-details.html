All Wushu Traning

Key Wushu Judge & Coach Training achievements highlighting my professional journey and certifications.

I am proud to have participated in the 22-day Wushu Judges & Coaches Training Program held at Bangladesh Krira Shikkha Protishtan (BKSP) in 2018. This prestigious training included four representatives from the Bangladesh Army and many highly skilled national-level athletes.

In 2016, I had the opportunity to attend the Wushu Judges & Coaches Training organized by the Bangladesh Wushu Federation. Successfully completing this program enabled me to achieve my official license, which became a significant milestone in my Wushu career.

Key Highlights

  • Certified Wushu Judge & Coach (Licensed)
  • Participation in BKSP 22-Day Training (2018)
  • Training by Bangladesh Wushu Federation (2016)
  • Represented in Ambassador Cup Wushu Demonstrations
  • Achieved Top Club Position in 2nd Ambassador Cup 2018
  • Experience in Coaching & Event Demonstrations
